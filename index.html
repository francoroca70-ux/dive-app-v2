Waivers
Owner only

This is a digital signature collection tool only. Seven Seas does not provide, verify, or take responsibility for waiver content or legal validity.

Enforceability of digital signatures varies by country and jurisdiction. Consult a local lawyer before relying on these documents legally. By enabling this feature you confirm your operation takes full responsibility for the legal validity of your forms.

Signed waivers are stored securely, but we recommend exporting each day's waivers to your own computer as a local backup — use the Export tool at the top of the Waivers page. It takes seconds and gives you a copy on hand whenever you need one.

Only the account owner can change this setting. Ask your owner or manager if you'd like Waivers turned on.
